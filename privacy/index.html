Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-10-25

This Privacy Policy describes how Zhao Chen (“SmartAlpha”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the SmartAlpha mobile application and related services (the “Service”).

Information We Collect

  • Account Identifiers: Firebase Authentication user ID (UID).
  • App Content: Watchlists, strategy parameters, subscription status stored in Firebase Firestore.
  • Purchases: Apple in-app purchase identifiers (product ID, transaction/receipt data) strictly for validating subscription status/eligibility.
  • Push Tokens: Device tokens (FCM) for notifications you opt into.
  • Diagnostics: Minimal logs related to function calls (timestamps, error codes, region) for reliability and security.
  • Market/News Data: Non-personal market/news data sourced from providers (e.g., Yahoo Finance, NewsAPI, GDELT).

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve core features (signals, news/event highlights, notifications).
  • Verify and enforce subscriptions and access to premium features.
  • Security, fraud prevention, debugging, and compliance.
  • Meet legal obligations.

Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We rely on third-party processors to operate the Service:

  • Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Cloud Functions, Cloud Messaging).
  • Apple (in-app purchases and subscription validation).
  • Market/news data providers (non-personal data only).

Data Retention

We retain information as long as needed to provide the Service and as required by law. Upon account deletion or when no longer necessary, we delete or de-identify data within a reasonable time.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data by contacting us or using in-app options where available.
  • Disable notifications in your device settings or in the app.

Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law. If we learn that we collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed outside your country. We take reasonable measures to protect it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective Date” and, where required, provide additional notice.
